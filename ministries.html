Ministries


Intercessory Ministry

The Intercessory Ministry consists of prayer warriors and mediators who continuously present the praises and petitions of the church, its members, and the community before God's throne.

Evangelism Ministry

The Evangelism Ministry has a twofold mission: actively spreading the gospel of Christ to non-believers and uplifting and strengthening fellow believers. This ministry is dedicated to both reaching out to the lost and nurturing the faith of believers.

Engineering Department

The Engineering Department serves as an overarching ministry responsible for the technical aspects of church operations. This includes maintaining and ensuring the proper functioning of all electronic and electrical equipment used during services, managing in-house recording studio facilities, recording and reproducing messages, and overseeing information systems within the church.

Drama Group

The Drama Group's objective is to convey the message of Jesus Christ through theatrical performances, drama sketches, and movies, effectively using the medium of drama to reach people with the gospel.

Choir Ministry

The Choir Ministry leads the congregation in worship through songs and special music during church gatherings. Their role is to create an atmosphere conducive to spiritual encounters, as stated in Psalm 22:3, where God inhabits the praises of His people.

Search The Scriptures Department

The Search The Scriptures Department, led by Sunday School Teachers, focuses on deepening the biblical knowledge of church members. Teachers prepare engaging Bible teachings to help believers understand and apply God's word effectively.

Ushering Ministry

The Ushering Ministry welcomes worshippers, assists them in finding seats, and ensures their comfort during church services. They also maintain order and minimize distractions to facilitate a conducive atmosphere for worship.

Follow-up Department

The Follow-up Department concentrates on nurturing new converts in the congregation, providing them with support, guidance, and church information to help them grow in their faith. Additionally, they reach out to existing members who have been absent for some time.

Hospitality Ministry

The Hospitality Ministry extends warmth and care to visitors and new members, offering them support and church information. They also conduct physical visits to members in need of encouragement and prayer.

Publications Ministry

The Publications Ministry produces literature that promotes commitment to Jesus Christ and reflects His lordship in daily life. This includes publishing Sunday service bulletins and other materials that communicate the church's vision and values effectively.

Security and Valet Department

The Security and Valet Department ensures safety and orderliness outside the church premises. They oversee parking lot management, safeguard members' vehicles and properties, and prioritize the security and well-being of all worshippers.
